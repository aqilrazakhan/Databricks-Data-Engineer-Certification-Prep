# Industry-Specific KPIs by Domain

Key Performance Indicators organized by business domain.

## E-Commerce & Retail

### Revenue Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** Sum of all sales
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** Total Revenue ÷ Total Orders
- **Revenue Per Visitor (RPV):** Total Revenue ÷ Total Visitors
- **Same-Store Sales Growth:** YoY comparison for existing locations

### Conversion Metrics
- **Conversion Rate:** Orders ÷ Total Visitors (Benchmark: 2-3% avg, 5%+ excellent)
- **Cart Abandonment Rate:** Abandoned Carts ÷ Total Carts (Benchmark: ~70% industry avg)
- **Add-to-Cart Rate:** Sessions with Cart Adds ÷ Total Sessions
- **Checkout Completion Rate:** Completed Checkouts ÷ Started Checkouts

### Customer Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):** Avg Revenue Per Customer × Avg Customer Lifespan
- **Repeat Purchase Rate:** Repeat Customers ÷ Total Customers (Benchmark: 20-40%)
- **Customer Retention Rate:** Retained Customers ÷ Previous Period Customers
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** % Promoters - % Detractors

### Product Metrics
- **Inventory Turnover:** Cost of Goods Sold ÷ Avg Inventory
- **Return Rate:** Returns ÷ Total Orders (Benchmark: 5-10% acceptable)
- **Units Per Transaction (UPT):** Total Units ÷ Total Transactions
- **Sell-Through Rate:** Units Sold ÷ Units Received

### Marketing Metrics
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Revenue from Ads ÷ Ad Spend (Benchmark: 4:1 min, 8:1+ excellent)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Clicks ÷ Impressions
- **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Ad Spend ÷ Clicks
- **Email Open Rate:** Emails Opened ÷ Emails Sent (Benchmark: 15-25%)

## Human Resources (HR)

### Workforce Metrics
- **Headcount:** Total number of employees
- **FTE (Full-Time Equivalent):** Total hours ÷ Standard full-time hours
- **Span of Control:** Direct Reports ÷ Managers
- **Employee-to-Manager Ratio:** Total Employees ÷ Total Managers

### Attrition & Retention
- **Attrition Rate:** Separations ÷ Avg Headcount (Benchmark: 10-15% annual)
- **Voluntary Turnover Rate:** Voluntary Exits ÷ Avg Headcount
- **Retention Rate:** (Headcount Start - Exits) ÷ Headcount Start
- **90-Day Turnover:** Exits within 90 days ÷ New Hires
- **Regrettable Turnover:** High-Performer Exits ÷ Total Exits

### Recruitment Metrics
- **Time-to-Hire:** Days from requisition to offer acceptance (Benchmark: 30-45 days)
- **Time-to-Fill:** Days from requisition to start date
- **Cost-Per-Hire:** Total Recruiting Costs ÷ Number of Hires
- **Offer Acceptance Rate:** Accepted Offers ÷ Total Offers (Benchmark: 85%+)
- **Quality of Hire:** Avg Performance Rating of New Hires
- **Source Effectiveness:** Hires per Source ÷ Applications per Source

### Diversity Metrics
- **Gender Diversity Ratio:** Female Employees ÷ Total Employees
- **Leadership Diversity:** Diverse Leaders ÷ Total Leaders
- **Pay Equity Ratio:** Avg Salary (Group A) ÷ Avg Salary (Group B)

### Performance Metrics
- **Performance Rating Distribution:** % in each rating category
- **Goal Completion Rate:** Completed Goals ÷ Total Goals
- **Training Hours Per Employee:** Total Training Hours ÷ Headcount

### Compensation Metrics
- **Compa-Ratio:** Actual Salary ÷ Midpoint of Salary Range (Benchmark: 0.85-1.15)
- **Salary Range Penetration:** (Salary - Min) ÷ (Max - Min)
- **Benefits Cost Per Employee:** Total Benefits Spend ÷ Headcount

## Finance & Accounting

### Revenue Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** Sum of all income
- **Recurring Revenue (MRR/ARR):** Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue
- **Revenue Growth Rate:** (Current - Previous) ÷ Previous
- **Revenue by Segment:** Revenue broken down by product/region/customer

### Profitability Metrics
- **Gross Profit:** Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold
- **Gross Margin %:** Gross Profit ÷ Revenue (Benchmark: 50-70% for SaaS)
- **Operating Profit (EBIT):** Revenue - Operating Expenses
- **Operating Margin %:** Operating Profit ÷ Revenue
- **Net Profit:** Revenue - All Expenses
- **Net Profit Margin %:** Net Profit ÷ Revenue
- **EBITDA:** Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization
- **EBITDA Margin %:** EBITDA ÷ Revenue

### Cash Flow Metrics
- **Operating Cash Flow:** Cash from operations
- **Free Cash Flow:** Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures
- **Cash Conversion Cycle:** Days Inventory + Days Receivable - Days Payable
- **Burn Rate:** Cash Spent per Month (for startups)
- **Runway:** Current Cash ÷ Monthly Burn Rate

### Budget & Variance
- **Budget Variance $:** Actual - Budget
- **Budget Variance %:** (Actual - Budget) ÷ Budget
- **Forecast Accuracy:** Actuals ÷ Forecast
- **Cost Center Performance:** Actual vs Budget by department

### Financial Ratios
- **Current Ratio:** Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities (Benchmark: 1.5-3.0)
- **Quick Ratio:** (Current Assets - Inventory) ÷ Current Liabilities
- **Debt-to-Equity:** Total Debt ÷ Total Equity
- **Return on Assets (ROA):** Net Income ÷ Total Assets
- **Return on Equity (ROE):** Net Income ÷ Shareholders' Equity

### Working Capital
- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO):** (Accounts Receivable ÷ Revenue) × 365
- **Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO):** (Inventory ÷ COGS) × 365
- **Days Payable Outstanding (DPO):** (Accounts Payable ÷ COGS) × 365

## Data Governance & Compliance

### Compliance Metrics
- **Overall Compliance Score:** Avg compliance across all regulations (%)
- **Regulation-Specific Scores:** Compliance % per regulation (GDPR, SOX, etc.)
- **Control Effectiveness:** Passing Controls ÷ Total Controls
- **Audit Findings:** Number of audit issues by severity
- **Remediation Rate:** Closed Issues ÷ Total Issues

### Data Quality Metrics
- **Completeness Score:** Non-Null Values ÷ Total Values
- **Accuracy Score:** Accurate Records ÷ Total Records
- **Consistency Score:** Consistent Values ÷ Total Values
- **Timeliness Score:** Current Records ÷ Total Records
- **Overall Data Quality Score:** Weighted average of quality dimensions

### PII & Sensitive Data
- **Total PII Records:** Count of personally identifiable information records
- **Unencrypted PII Records:** PII without encryption
- **PII at Risk:** PII with inadequate protection
- **Sensitive Data Coverage:** Protected Records ÷ Total Sensitive Records
- **Data Retention Violations:** Records exceeding retention policy

### Access & Security
- **Access Violations:** Unauthorized access attempts
- **Failed Login Attempts:** Failed logins (threshold: >3 = alert)
- **Privileged Access Accounts:** Count of admin-level accounts
- **Access Review Completion:** Completed Reviews ÷ Required Reviews
- **Orphaned Accounts:** Active accounts for terminated users

### Incident Management
- **Open Incidents:** Active incidents by severity
- **Mean Time to Detect (MTTD):** Avg time to discover incident
- **Mean Time to Respond (MTTR):** Avg time to respond to incident
- **Mean Time to Resolve:** Avg time to close incident
- **Incident Recurrence Rate:** Recurring Issues ÷ Total Incidents
- **Breach Cost Avoidance:** Estimated $ saved by prevention

### Risk Assessment
- **High-Risk Data Assets:** Assets with risk score > threshold
- **Risk Score Distribution:** Count by risk level (Low/Med/High)
- **Third-Party Risk:** Vendor compliance scores
- **Data Breach Likelihood:** Probability score based on controls

## SaaS & Subscription Businesses

### Revenue Metrics
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** Sum of monthly subscription revenue
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** MRR × 12
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** MRR ÷ Total Users
- **Revenue Churn Rate:** Lost MRR ÷ Starting MRR

### Growth Metrics
- **Net New MRR:** New MRR + Expansion MRR - Churned MRR
- **MRR Growth Rate:** (Current MRR - Previous MRR) ÷ Previous MRR
- **Quick Ratio:** (New MRR + Expansion MRR) ÷ Churned MRR (Benchmark: 4+ excellent)

### Customer Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Sales & Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** ARPU × Avg Customer Lifespan
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** LTV ÷ CAC (Benchmark: 3:1 minimum, 5:1+ excellent)
- **Payback Period:** CAC ÷ (ARPU × Gross Margin) (Benchmark: <12 months)

### Churn Metrics
- **Customer Churn Rate:** Lost Customers ÷ Starting Customers (Benchmark: <5% monthly)
- **Revenue Churn Rate:** Lost MRR ÷ Starting MRR
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR):** (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn) ÷ Starting MRR (Benchmark: 100%+, 120%+ excellent)

### Engagement Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Unique users per day
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** Unique users per month
- **DAU/MAU Ratio:** DAU ÷ MAU (Benchmark: 20%+ engaged)
- **Feature Adoption Rate:** Users Using Feature ÷ Total Users

## Manufacturing & Operations

### Production Metrics
- **Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE):** Availability × Performance × Quality
- **Capacity Utilization:** Actual Output ÷ Maximum Capacity
- **Throughput:** Units produced per time period
- **Cycle Time:** Time to complete one unit

### Quality Metrics
- **First Pass Yield (FPY):** Units Passed First Time ÷ Total Units
- **Defect Rate:** Defective Units ÷ Total Units
- **Scrap Rate:** Scrapped Units ÷ Total Units
- **Rework Rate:** Reworked Units ÷ Total Units

### Inventory Metrics
- **Inventory Turnover:** COGS ÷ Avg Inventory
- **Days Inventory:** (Avg Inventory ÷ COGS) × 365
- **Stockout Rate:** Stockouts ÷ Total Orders
- **Inventory Accuracy:** Actual Inventory ÷ System Inventory

### Maintenance Metrics
- **Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF):** Operating Time ÷ Number of Failures
- **Mean Time To Repair (MTTR):** Total Repair Time ÷ Number of Repairs
- **Planned Maintenance %:** Planned ÷ Total Maintenance Hours

## Sales & CRM

### Pipeline Metrics
- **Pipeline Value:** Sum of all opportunity values
- **Pipeline Coverage:** Pipeline Value ÷ Sales Target
- **Weighted Pipeline:** Sum of (Opportunity Value × Win Probability)

### Conversion Metrics
- **Lead-to-Opportunity Conversion:** Opportunities ÷ Leads
- **Opportunity-to-Win Conversion:** Won Deals ÷ Total Opportunities (Benchmark: 20-30%)
- **Win Rate:** Won Deals ÷ (Won + Lost Deals)

### Sales Activity
- **Average Deal Size:** Total Revenue ÷ Number of Deals
- **Sales Cycle Length:** Avg days from lead to closed-won
- **Activities Per Rep:** Total Activities ÷ Number of Sales Reps
- **Quota Attainment:** Actual Sales ÷ Quota (Benchmark: 80%+ team average)

### Customer Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Sales & Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers
- **Sales Efficiency (Magic Number):** Net New ARR ÷ Sales & Marketing Spend
- **Logo Retention:** Retained Customers ÷ Starting Customers
