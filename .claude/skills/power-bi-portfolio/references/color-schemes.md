# Professional Dashboard Color Schemes

Curated color palettes for Power BI dashboards.

## Default Professional Blue

**Best for:** All industries, executive dashboards, conservative clients

```
Primary Blue:      #1F4E78  ■  Dark Professional Blue (headers, primary)
Accent Blue:       #00B0F0  ■  Bright Blue (highlights, links)
Accent Orange:     #FFC000  ■  Golden Orange (secondary highlights)
Success Green:     #70AD47  ■  Growth Green (positive trends)
Warning Red:       #C00000  ■  Alert Red (negative trends, warnings)
Neutral Gray:      #404040  ■  Dark Gray (text)
Light Gray:        #F2F2F2  ■  Light Gray (background)
White:             #FFFFFF  ■  White (cards, panels)
```

**Usage:**
- Headers/Titles: Primary Blue
- KPI Cards Background: White
- Positive Trends: Success Green
- Negative Trends: Warning Red
- Accent Elements: Accent Blue or Accent Orange
- Page Background: Light Gray
- Text: Neutral Gray

## Color Usage Guidelines

### KPI Cards
- **Background:** White or Light Gray
- **Value Text:** Primary Color or Neutral Gray
- **Trend Icon:** Success Green (up) or Warning Red (down)
- **Border:** Light Gray or no border

### Charts
- **Single Series:** Use Primary Color
- **Multiple Series:** Use Primary + Accent colors
- **Comparison:** Primary vs Secondary color
- **Trends Over Time:** Single color with gradient

### Conditional Formatting

**3-Color Scale (Bad → Good):**
- Low: Warning Red (#C00000)
- Mid: Accent Orange (#FFC000)
- High: Success Green (#70AD47)

**2-Color Scale:**
- Below Target: Warning Red
- Above Target: Success Green

**Traffic Lights:**
- Red: #C00000
- Yellow: #FFC000
- Green: #70AD47

### Accessibility Considerations

**Contrast Ratios (WCAG 2.1 AA):**
- Normal text: 4.5:1 minimum
- Large text (18pt+): 3:1 minimum
- UI elements: 3:1 minimum

**Color Blindness:**
- Don't rely on color alone
- Use patterns, icons, or labels
- Avoid red-green only combinations
- Test with color blindness simulators

### Visual Hierarchy

**Importance → Color Intensity:**
1. Most Important: Primary Color (darkest)
2. Important: Accent Color (bright)
3. Supporting: Light Gray
4. Background: Lightest Gray

## Application Examples

### Executive Summary Page
- Page Background: Light Gray (#F2F2F2)
- KPI Cards: White with Primary Blue headers
- Revenue Trend Chart: Primary Blue line
- Category Donut: Accent Blue, Accent Orange, Success Green rotation
- Top Products Bar: Primary Blue bars

### Comparison Pages
- Actual: Primary Blue
- Target/Budget: Light Gray or dotted line
- Variance Positive: Success Green
- Variance Negative: Warning Red

### Heat Maps
- Low Value: Light Gray
- Medium Value: Accent Orange
- High Value: Primary Blue or Success Green
- Negative: Warning Red
